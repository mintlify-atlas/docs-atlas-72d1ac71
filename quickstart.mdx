## For Tourists

### Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Visit the Kin Conecta homepage and click **Registrarse** (Sign Up) in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Tourist Role">
    Choose **Turista** from the role selection screen. This unlocks features for browsing guides and booking tours.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Fill in your registration details:
    
    - **Full name**: Your complete name
    - **Date of birth**: Required for age verification
    - **Country code**: Select your phone's country code (e.g., +52 for Mexico)
    - **Phone number**: 10-digit mobile number
    - **Email**: Your primary email address
    - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure your email is valid - you'll need it to verify your account and receive booking confirmations.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Set Up Your Tourist Profile

Once registered, enhance your profile to get better tour recommendations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Travel Preferences" icon="heart">
    Tell us what kind of traveler you are:
    
    - **Travel Style**: Cultural, Gastronomic, Adventure, Nature, Historical
    - **Activity Level**: Low, Moderate, or High intensity
    - **Group Preference**: Solo, couples, small groups, or large groups
    - **Planning Level**: How much detail you prefer in itineraries
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Languages & Interests" icon="language">
    - Select languages you speak (Spanish, English, French, German, Italian)
    - Choose your interests: Culture, Gastronomy, Adventure, Nature, Photography
    - Add dietary preferences if applicable
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Profile Photo" icon="image">
    Upload a profile photo to personalize your account. This helps guides recognize you during tours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Find Your Perfect Tour

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the Explore Page">
    Navigate to **Explorar** from your dashboard sidebar. Here you'll find:
    
    - Featured destinations across Mexico
    - Top-rated guides and tours
    - Search functionality by destination, guide, or activity type
    - Category filters: Gastronomía, Aventura, Historia, Cultura
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search and Filter">
    Use the search bar to find specific experiences:
    
    ```plaintext
    Search examples:
    - "cenotes Puerto Morelos"
    - "food tour Guadalajara"
    - "historic center CDMX"
    - "colonial architecture Mérida"
    ```
    
    Filter results by categories to narrow down options.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Tour Details">
    Click on any tour to view:
    
    - **Duration**: Typical tours run 4 hours
    - **Price**: Listed in MXN (Mexican Pesos)
    - **Group size**: Maximum number of participants
    - **Meeting point**: Where the tour starts
    - **Included items**: What's provided (guide, entrance fees, tastings, equipment)
    - **Guide profile**: Experience level, languages, certifications, ratings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Guide Compatibility">
    Review the guide's profile:
    
    - **Rating**: Average from previous tourists
    - **Reviews**: Read experiences from other travelers
    - **Tour style**: Intensity level, group dynamics, pace
    - **Languages**: Communication options
    - **Certifications**: Professional qualifications
    - **Adaptations**: Special accommodations offered
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 4: Book Your Experience

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Date and Time">
    Choose your preferred booking date. The system will show available time slots based on the guide's calendar.
    
    <Note>
      Tours are booked in 4-hour blocks. Plan accordingly for travel time to the meeting point.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Special Notes">
    Include any special requests or requirements:
    
    - Dietary restrictions (gluten-free, vegetarian, allergies)
    - Mobility considerations
    - Specific interests to prioritize
    - Group composition details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Confirm">
    Double-check your booking details:
    
    - Tour name and guide
    - Date, time, and meeting point
    - Total price and included items
    - Cancellation policy
    
    Submit your booking request. The status will be **pending** until the guide confirms.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    You'll receive a notification when the guide:
    
    - **Confirms** your booking (status: confirmed)
    - **Requests changes** to date/time (status: change_requested)
    - Declines due to unavailability
    
    <Tip>
      Most guides respond within 1-3 hours during business hours.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 5: Communicate and Prepare

Once confirmed, you can:

- **Message your guide** directly through the chat system
- Ask questions about what to bring, wear, or expect
- Coordinate pickup details if applicable
- View the meeting point on your trip details

### Step 6: Enjoy Your Tour

On tour day:

1. Arrive at the meeting point 5-10 minutes early
2. Look for your guide (their profile photo is in your booking)
3. Introduce yourself and confirm the itinerary
4. Enjoy your authentic local experience!

### Step 7: Leave a Review

After your tour:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rate Your Experience">
    Navigate to **Mis Viajes** (My Trips) and select the completed tour. Rate your experience from 1-5 stars.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write a Review">
    Share your thoughts to help other tourists:
    
    - What you loved about the experience
    - How the guide performed
    - Accuracy of the tour description
    - Value for money
    
    <Tip>
      Detailed reviews help guides improve and assist other tourists in making informed decisions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Guides

### Step 1: Create Your Guide Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Visit the Kin Conecta homepage and click **Registrarse** (Sign Up).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Guide Role">
    Choose **Guía** from the role selection screen. This gives you access to tour management and booking tools.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Provide your personal information:
    
    - Full name
    - Date of birth
    - Country code and phone number
    - Email address
    - Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Build Your Guide Profile

Your profile is your storefront - make it compelling!

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Professional Information" icon="briefcase">
    Complete your professional details:
    
    - **Summary**: Brief introduction (50-100 words)
    - **Story**: Your background and guiding philosophy (200+ words)
    - **Status text**: Current availability (e.g., "Available this week", "Booking 2 weeks ahead")
    - **Hourly rate**: Your base rate in MXN
    - **Experience level**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Senior
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Service Details" icon="list-check">
    Define your tour style:
    
    - **Tour intensity**: Low (relaxed), Moderate, or High (active)
    - **Group size**: Preferred number of participants (e.g., "2-6 people", "4-10 people")
    - **Tour style**: Your guiding approach (e.g., "Historical narrative with photo stops")
    - **Transport offered**: Walking, metro, van, private vehicle
    - **Photo style**: Architecture, food, portraits, nature, street photography
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Languages & Expertise" icon="globe">
    - Add all languages you speak fluently
    - Select expertise areas:
      - Local History
      - Gastronomy Tours
      - Colonial Architecture
      - Urban Art
      - Nature & Cenotes
      - Cultural Experiences
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Locations & Certifications" icon="award">
    - **Locations**: Cities and areas where you guide
    - **Certifications**: Professional credentials (e.g., "Certified Cultural Guide CDMX", "Food Safety Certification", "First Aid Certified")
    - **Adaptations**: Special accommodations you offer (e.g., "Flexible pace for seniors", "Vegetarian options", "Bilingual support")
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Photos & Branding" icon="camera">
    Upload high-quality images:
    
    - **Profile photo**: Professional headshot showing your face clearly
    - **Cover photo**: Showcase your best tour moments or destinations
    - **Tour photos**: Images of locations, food, or experiences you offer
    
    <Tip>
      High-quality photos increase your booking rate by up to 70%. Use natural lighting and show authentic moments.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Create Your First Tour

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Tour Management">
    From your guide dashboard, navigate to **Tours** in the sidebar and click **Create New Tour**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Basic Tour Information">
    Fill in the essential details:
    
    ```yaml
    Title: "Historic Center and Museums Walk"
    Category: Historia y cultura
    Description: Detailed tour description including highlights, 
                 what makes it unique, and what tourists will learn
    Duration: 4.0 hours
    Max group size: 8 people
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pricing and Logistics">
    Set your tour parameters:
    
    - **Price**: Total tour price in MXN (not per person - clarify in description)
    - **Currency**: MXN (Mexican Peso)
    - **Meeting point**: Specific, easy-to-find location (e.g., "Palacio de Bellas Artes main entrance")
    - **Cover image**: Eye-catching photo representing the tour
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="What's Included">
    List everything included in the tour price:
    
    <CodeGroup>
    ```plaintext Example: Cultural Tour
    - Bilingual guide service
    - Basic entrance fees to 2 museums
    - Printed historical timeline
    - Water and snacks
    ```
    
    ```plaintext Example: Food Tour
    - Expert food guide
    - Tastings at 5 locations
    - Market access and stories
    - Recipe card for 1 dish
    ```
    
    ```plaintext Example: Nature Tour
    - Certified nature guide
    - Basic equipment (snorkel, life vest)
    - Hydration drinks
    - Transportation from meeting point
    - First aid kit on hand
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Destinations">
    Link your tour to destination(s):
    
    - Choose from existing destinations in the system
    - Multiple destinations can be selected for multi-city tours
    - This helps tourists find your tour when searching by location
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Tour Status">
    Choose your tour status:
    
    - **Draft**: Not visible to tourists (work in progress)
    - **Pending**: Submitted for review (if platform requires approval)
    - **Active**: Live and bookable by tourists
    - **Inactive**: Temporarily unavailable
    
    <Warning>
      Start with "Draft" to review everything before making it live. You can activate it later.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 4: Manage Your Calendar

Keep your availability up to date:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar View">
    Navigate to **Calendario** in your dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Block Unavailable Times">
    Mark dates when you're unavailable:
    
    - Personal time off
    - Other commitments
    - Fully booked days
    
    This prevents double-booking and sets tourist expectations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Booking Requests">
    When tourists book your tours:
    
    - You'll receive a notification
    - Review the booking details and tourist profile
    - Check for any special requests in the notes
    - Confirm or request changes within 24 hours
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 5: Communicate with Tourists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Respond to Messages">
    Tourists can message you before and after booking. Quick responses improve your rating and booking rate.
    
    <Tip>
      Guides who respond within 1 hour have 3x higher confirmation rates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Tour Details">
    Before the tour, clarify:
    
    - Exact meeting time and location
    - What to bring (comfortable shoes, hat, sunscreen, etc.)
    - Any special considerations
    - Weather contingencies
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 6: Deliver Amazing Tours

On tour day:

1. **Arrive early** at the meeting point
2. **Greet your tourists** warmly and confirm the group
3. **Set expectations** for the tour (duration, stops, pace)
4. **Engage actively** with storytelling and local insights
5. **Be flexible** to group needs and interests
6. **Encourage questions** and interaction
7. **End professionally** with recommendations for their continued journey

### Step 7: Manage Post-Tour

After each tour:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update Trip Status">
    Mark the trip as **completed** in your dashboard. This triggers:
    
    - Payment processing (if integrated)
    - Tourist review request
    - Trip appears in your history
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request and Respond to Reviews">
    - Encourage tourists to leave reviews
    - Respond to reviews (especially constructive feedback)
    - Use feedback to improve future tours
    
    <Note>
      Your average rating and review count directly impact your visibility in search results.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Your Performance">
    Monitor your guide dashboard:
    
    - **Rating average**: Keep above 4.5 stars
    - **Reviews count**: More reviews = more trust
    - **Bookings count**: Track your growth
    - **Response time**: Aim for under 2 hours
  </Step>
</Steps>
