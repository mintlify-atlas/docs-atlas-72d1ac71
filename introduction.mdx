## Connecting Travelers, Illuminating Destinations

Kin Conecta is a travel social network that bridges the gap between tourists seeking authentic experiences and local guides who know their destinations best. The name "Kin" comes from the Mayan word for "Sun," representing connection, energy, and the warmth of human interaction.

### What is Kin Conecta?

Kin Conecta addresses a common problem in modern tourism: standardized, generic travel experiences. Our platform enables direct connections between:

- **Tourists** (national and international) looking for personalized, authentic experiences
- **Local guides** in Mexico offering unique insights and curated tours

## Key Features

Kin Conecta offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to facilitate meaningful connections between tourists and guides:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intelligent Matching Algorithm" icon="brain-circuit">
    Our proprietary matching algorithm analyzes compatibility between tourists and guides based on experience level, expectations, pace, interests, and logistics to ensure the best possible pairings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="User Roles & Authentication" icon="users">
    Secure authentication system supporting two primary roles: Tourists who browse and book experiences, and Guides who create and manage tours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Tour Management" icon="map">
    Comprehensive tour creation and management system with support for categories, destinations, itineraries, and availability tracking.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Booking System" icon="calendar-check">
    Complete trip booking workflow with status tracking, payment processing, and automated notifications.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Real-time Messaging" icon="messages">
    Direct communication channel between tourists and guides to discuss trip details and logistics.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reviews & Ratings" icon="star">
    Post-tour review system allowing tourists to rate their experiences and guides to build reputation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technology Stack

Kin Conecta is built on a modern, robust technology stack:

- **Backend**: Java (JDK 21) with Spring Boot 4
- **Database**: MySQL 8.0 with relational data model
- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla), Bootstrap 5
- **Architecture**: MVC pattern evolving toward RESTful services
