## Welcome to Kin Conecta

Kin Conecta connects local guides with travelers seeking authentic experiences. This guide will help you get started as a guide on the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Guide Profile">
    Begin by setting up your professional guide profile. Your profile is your digital storefront and the first thing tourists see.

    ### Required Information

    - **Full Name**: Your professional name as it will appear to tourists
    - **Profile Summary**: A brief introduction (20+ characters)
    - **Professional Story**: Share your background and passion for guiding
    - **Profile Photo**: Upload a clear, professional photo
    - **Cover Image**: Choose an image that represents your guiding style

    <Note>
      Your profile is stored locally and synced with the backend API. Make sure to complete all required fields before publishing.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Professional Details">
    Add the information that helps tourists understand your expertise and style.

    ### Key Sections to Complete

    **Areas of Experience**
    ```javascript
    // Example areas from dashboard-profile-edit.js
    [
      "Historia y patrimonio urbano",
      "Gastronomía local y mercados tradicionales",
      "Arte contemporáneo y cultura popular"
    ]
    ```

    **Locations You Cover**
    - List all neighborhoods and areas you guide in
    - Be specific (e.g., "Centro Histórico", "Coyoacán", "Roma-Condesa")
    - Add up to 10 locations for better discoverability

    **Languages**
    - List all languages you speak
    - Include proficiency level (e.g., "Inglés (C1)", "Español (nativo)")

    **Experience Level**
    - Choose your experience category
    - Options: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
    - Include years of experience

    <Tip>
      Guides with complete profiles receive 3x more booking requests than those with incomplete profiles.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Tour Style and Preferences">
    Define how you work to attract the right tourists for your style.

    ### Tour Preferences

    | Field | Description | Example |
    |-------|-------------|----------|
    | **Guiding Style** | Your approach and personality | "Estilo narrativo, cercano y dinámico" |
    | **Group Size** | Maximum capacity | "Hasta 10 personas (ideal: 6)" |
    | **Tour Intensity** | Physical activity level | "Moderada: caminatas de 5 a 7 km" |
    | **Transport Offered** | Available transportation | "Caminata guiada, transporte público" |

    ### Certifications and Adaptations

    **Add Your Certifications**
    - First aid training
    - Tourism guide licenses
    - Language certifications
    - Specialized training

    **Accessibility Adaptations**
    ```javascript
    // From dashboard-profile-edit.js
    [
      "Rutas alternativas para movilidad reducida",
      "Ritmo adaptable para familias con menores"
    ]
    ```

    <Warning>
      Be honest about physical requirements and accessibility. This helps set proper expectations and prevents disappointed tourists.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Your Availability">
    Set your hourly rate and availability status.

    ### Pricing
    - Set your hourly rate in USD or local currency
    - Consider your experience level and local market rates
    - Include what's covered in your rate

    ### Availability Status
    The system tracks your availability status:
    - **Available Today** - Ready to accept bookings
    - **Booking in Advance** - Require advance notice
    - **Limited Availability** - Specific dates only

    ```javascript
    // Status display from dashboard-profile.js
    statusText: "DISPONIBLE HOY"
    hourlyRate: "$40 USD / hora"
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Publish">
    Before going live, review all your information.

    ### Pre-Launch Checklist

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Profile Completeness" icon="user">
        - ✅ Name and summary
        - ✅ Professional photos
        - ✅ Contact information
        - ✅ Language preferences
      </Card>

      <Card title="Professional Details" icon="certificate">
        - ✅ Experience areas
        - ✅ Locations covered
        - ✅ Certifications added
        - ✅ Tour style defined
      </Card>

      <Card title="Tour Preferences" icon="hiking">
        - ✅ Group size limits
        - ✅ Intensity levels
        - ✅ Transport options
        - ✅ Special adaptations
      </Card>

      <Card title="Availability" icon="calendar">
        - ✅ Hourly rate set
        - ✅ Status configured
        - ✅ Calendar synced
        - ✅ Ready to accept bookings
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Note>
      Profile changes are saved locally and synced with the backend API. Updates appear immediately on your public profile.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
