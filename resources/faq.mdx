## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Kin Conecta?">
    Kin Conecta is a travel social network platform that connects tourists (both national and international) with verified local guides in Mexico. The name "Kin" comes from Maya and means "Sun", representing connection, energy, and human closeness.

    Our platform enables authentic travel experiences by facilitating direct connections between travelers seeking personalized adventures and local experts who know their regions intimately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Kin Conecta differ from other travel platforms?">
    Unlike generic travel booking sites, Kin Conecta focuses on:

    - **Direct connections** between tourists and local guides
    - **Personalized experiences** tailored to individual preferences
    - **Verified local expertise** ensuring authentic cultural immersion
    - **Intelligent matching** based on interests, pace, travel style, and preferences
    - **Community-driven** approach with reviews and ratings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Kin Conecta available in my language?">
    The platform currently supports Spanish and English. Guide profiles indicate the languages they speak, making it easy to find guides who can communicate in your preferred language.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What regions of Mexico does Kin Conecta cover?">
    Kin Conecta covers destinations throughout Mexico, including major cities, cultural centers, beach destinations, and off-the-beaten-path locations. Use our search filters to find guides in your destination of interest.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Tourists

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I find the right guide for my trip?">
    Finding your perfect guide is easy with our intelligent matching system:

    1. **Create your tourist profile** with your interests, travel style, and preferences
    2. **Search by destination** using our location filters
    3. **Browse matched guides** - our algorithm shows the most compatible guides first based on:
       - Shared languages
       - Matching interests and expertise
       - Compatible travel pace and style
       - Group size preferences
       - Transportation options
       - Photo preferences and accessibility needs
    4. **Review profiles** including ratings, reviews, and available tour plans
    5. **Contact guides** directly to discuss your specific needs

    <Note>
      The matching score (0-100) helps you identify guides with the highest compatibility based on your profile preferences.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information should I include in my tourist profile?">
    A complete profile helps our matching algorithm find the best guides for you:

    - **Location**: Where you're traveling to
    - **Languages**: Languages you speak
    - **Travel style**: Cultural, gastronomic, adventure, eco-tourism, etc.
    - **Interests**: Specific activities and experiences you enjoy
    - **Trip type**: City break, weekend getaway, extended vacation, etc.
    - **Pace & company**: Solo traveler, couple, family, group; relaxed or intensive pace
    - **Activity level**: Low, moderate, or high physical activity
    - **Group preferences**: Small groups, private tours, larger groups
    - **Dietary preferences**: Any dietary restrictions or preferences
    - **Planning level**: How much planning and structure you prefer
    - **Transport preferences**: Walking, public transit, car, etc.
    - **Photo preferences**: Your photography interests
    - **Accessibility needs**: Any mobility or accessibility requirements
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I book a tour with a guide?">
    The booking process:

    1. **Browse or search** for guides in your destination
    2. **Review tour plans** published by guides
    3. **Contact the guide** to discuss details and availability
    4. **Confirm the booking** - guides will provide specific instructions
    5. **Track your booking** status through your dashboard

    <Note>
      Future versions will include integrated chat/messaging functionality for seamless communication.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I save my favorite guides or tours?">
    Yes! Use the favorites feature to save:
    - **Favorite guides** for easy access later
    - **Favorite tours** to build your travel wishlist

    Access your saved favorites from your profile dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I leave a review for a guide?">
    After completing a tour:

    1. Navigate to the guide's profile
    2. Click "Write a Review"
    3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
    4. Share details about:
       - What you enjoyed most
       - The guide's expertise and communication
       - Whether the experience met your expectations
       - Any suggestions for future travelers

    Your honest reviews help other tourists make informed decisions and help guides improve their services.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need to cancel or reschedule a tour?">
    Contact your guide directly as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. Each guide may have different cancellation policies, which should be discussed when booking.

    Check the trip status history to track any changes to your booking.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Guides

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I become a verified guide on Kin Conecta?">
    To become a verified guide:

    1. **Register** for a guide account
    2. **Complete your guide profile** with detailed information
    3. **Add certifications** (if applicable) - tourism certifications, licenses, first aid training, etc.
    4. **Specify your expertise areas** - cultural heritage, gastronomy, adventure sports, etc.
    5. **List your service locations** - cities and regions where you offer tours
    6. **Create your first tour plan** to showcase your offerings
    7. **Submit for verification** - our team will review your profile

    <Note>
      Verified guides receive a verification badge on their profile, increasing trust with potential tourists.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What should I include in my guide profile?">
    A comprehensive profile attracts more tourists:

    - **Professional photos**: Avatar and cover images
    - **Bio**: Your experience, passion, and local knowledge
    - **Languages**: All languages you speak fluently
    - **Expertise areas**: Your specialties (history, food, nature, etc.)
    - **Service locations**: Where you offer tours
    - **Certifications**: Professional credentials and training
    - **Tour preferences**: Group sizes, pace, activity levels you accommodate
    - **Transportation options**: What you can provide
    - **Photo services**: If you offer photography during tours
    - **Accessibility**: Special accommodations you can provide

    The more complete your profile, the better our matching algorithm can connect you with compatible tourists.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create and publish tour plans?">
    Tour plans showcase your offerings:

    1. Navigate to **Tour Management** in your dashboard
    2. Click **Create New Tour**
    3. Include:
       - Tour title and description
       - Destination(s) covered
       - Duration and schedule
       - Categories (cultural, gastronomic, adventure, etc.)
       - Price and what's included
       - Maximum group size
       - Required fitness level
       - What to bring
       - Meeting point details
    4. **Add photos** to make your tour visually appealing
    5. **Publish** when ready

    You can create multiple tour plans for different experiences and destinations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the matching algorithm work for guides?">
    The matching system helps you find compatible tourists:

    - Tourists whose interests align with your expertise
    - Language compatibility
    - Matching travel pace and style preferences
    - Compatible group size preferences
    - Similar planning and logistics approaches

    Check your **Matched Tourists** section to see travelers who are highly compatible with your offerings (sorted by match score 0-100).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I manage bookings and availability?">
    Manage your tours through the dashboard:

    - **View trip bookings** - see all current and upcoming tours
    - **Update trip status** - confirmed, in-progress, completed, cancelled
    - **Track status history** - maintain records of all booking changes
    - **Manage availability** - update your calendar and tour availability

    Stay responsive to tourist inquiries to build a strong reputation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can tourists leave reviews of my services?">
    Yes, tourists can leave reviews and ratings after completing tours with you. Reviews help:
    - Build your reputation and credibility
    - Attract more tourists
    - Provide valuable feedback for improvement
    - Improve your match score with similar tourists

    You can also **reply to reviews** to show appreciation or address concerns professionally.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get paid for my services?">
    Currently, payment arrangements are made directly between guides and tourists. Kin Conecta facilitates the connection, but financial transactions are handled independently.

    <Note>
      Discuss payment terms, methods, and cancellation policies clearly with tourists before confirming bookings.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
    Kin Conecta works best on modern browsers:
    - Google Chrome (recommended)
    - Mozilla Firefox
    - Safari
    - Microsoft Edge

    Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version for optimal performance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Currently, Kin Conecta is a web-based platform accessible through mobile browsers. The interface is responsive and mobile-friendly.

    A dedicated mobile app is planned for future development.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is my data protected?">
    Kin Conecta takes data security seriously:
    - Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
    - User data is stored securely in MySQL databases
    - Authentication systems validate all user sessions
    - Personal information is not shared with third parties

    See our [Contact Support](/resources/contact-support) page if you have specific privacy concerns.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
